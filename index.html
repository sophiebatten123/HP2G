Who We Are

HP2g Ltd is a mechanical engineering company specialising in commercial heating, plumbing and gas systems, we are, primarily, a maintenance company specialising in supporting schools, factories, and healthcare establishments.

Formed in 2008 by two engineers who had worked together for a number of years previously, they both felt that a bespoke service was required for their clients who needed, or would benefit from, a one stop shop for gas appliance servicing and reactive call outs for heating, plumbing and gas issues.

We are gas safe registered and fully qualified to work on a range of gas appliances and can carry out gas testing and fault finding on large installations that use both natural and liquid petroleum gas.

What We Do

Primarily we are a maintenance company, we maintain commercial gas appliances such as boilers, high level gas fired heaters, also plumbing and water systems and commercial gas pipework systems providing specific written reporting detailing appliance condition and remedial works that may be required. But we also provide the essential support to each client of a call out service, should the boilers break down or the heating system develop a fault we are available to attend, and fault find until the issue is rectified.
