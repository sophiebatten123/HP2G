What We Do

Primarily we are a maintenance company, we maintain commercial gas appliances such as boilers, high level gas fired heaters, also plumbing and water systems and commercial gas pipework systems providing specific written reporting detailing appliance condition and remedial works that may be required. But we also provide the essential support to each client of a call out service, should the boilers break down or the heating system develop a fault we are available to attend, and fault find until the issue is rectified.

All our clients have plumbing and hot water systems, we monitor the functionality of our clients’ systems to ensure that they are functioning correctly this might include checking TMV3 blender valves used to blend hot water temperatures down to a safe level for handwashing purposes also ensuring that cold water is being stored correctly and that the temperatures are correct as the water issues from the tap.

Commercial gas testing is a specialist field, not many engineers have the qualifications required to work on the largest gas installations, but our engineers do, we’re able to investigate any gas issue to any size which some of our clients find very useful as each year we can carry out a full gas tightness test of the whole site or investigate suspected gas leaks without the need to subcontract work to unknown contractors.

Our Work

A Tailored Bespoke Service

We also carry out installation work which might include the fitting of replacement boilers, high level heaters, pumps and associated heating plant, water heaters, calorifiers, pipework and heat emitters such as radiators and fan convectors.

So, we offer a one stop, bespoke service to our clients for commercial gas appliance servicing, plumbing, heating, and gas issues along with a reactive call out service. We develop a close working relationship with all our clients creating a deep understanding of the customers site over time to ensure that we’re able to locate and fix any issue quickly and efficiently.

As we work in education and healthcare establishments all of our engineers are enhanced DBS checked with many years’ experience working in such environments, we’re WRAS approved and are proud to be accredited with Safecontractor reassuring all of our clients that we have robust management systems in place to demonstrate that our paperwork is compliant with current legislation and that processes are in place to ensure health and safety is handled in the best way possible.
